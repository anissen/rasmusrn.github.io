About

My name is Rasmus. I love writing game software from scratch and doing my best to get the details just right. This approach is painstakingly but worth it. Firstly, it forces me to learn wonderful ideas and concepts about subjects I'm very passionate about. And secondly, this mindset leads to products I'm deeply proud of.

Doing things properly, no matter how hard, is what makes me happy.

I'm interested in entity/component architectures, AI/path finding, physics simulation, rendering, and realtime network synchronization. I like spending time pondering design issues about how we can connect all these systems in useful and elegant ways.

On the Binsai blog, I write about my latest experiments and thoughts.
